Knowing

"What will happen when they run out of numbers?"

After opening the "time capsule", in which in 1959 a group of students placed drawings with their vision of the future, in the hands of Professor John Kestler gets a mysterious sheet, from top to bottom written in numbers. In search of a transcript of the sheet, kestler establishes a mysterious link between the numbers and the world's largest disasters that have occurred on Earth over the past 50 years. If you believe the figures, tragedies can not be avoided in the future, only now kestler knows when to expect them. But is it possible to prevent them? And most importantly, what will happen when the chain of numbers is over?

Genre: Mystery, Drama

Release date: March 20, 2009
